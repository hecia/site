​HEC IA is launching a new series of technical workshops around AI, starting strong: deploy your own ChatGPT in one session.

​What you'll build: Create and deploy your own personal AI chat interface (OpenWebUI) on a remote server in the cloud, accessible from anywhere. You'll understand the entire deployment process of a modern application.

## ​Why it matters:

​You'll discover containerization (Docker), the technology that powers the cloud around us

​A concrete entry point into self-hosting and taking back control of your tools

​Useful if you want to de-GAFAM yourself and host your own services

​Technical prerequisites: NONE. Literally zero. No installation, no prior setup. Just you, your laptop, and an internet connection. The workshop is designed to be accessible to everyone, even if you've never touched code before.
